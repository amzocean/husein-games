🎲 Game Overview

Objective: Be the first player to complete 3 full property sets on the table.

Players: 2–5 (use 2 decks for 6+)

Setup: Remove 4 rules cards, shuffle, deal 5 cards each, remaining = draw pile.

Turn: Draw 2 cards → Play up to 3 cards → Discard to 7 max hand.

Three play zones: (1) Bank (money/action cards face-up), (2) Property area, (3) Discard pile (used actions).

⚡ Action Cards (34 total)

💰 Pass Go

10 cards • Bank value: $1M

Draw 2 extra cards from the draw pile. Can play multiple per turn (watch hand limit).

💳 Debt Collector

3 cards • Bank value: $3M

Force ONE opponent to pay you $5M. They choose how to pay (bank, property, or combo).

🎂 It's My Birthday

3 cards • Bank value: $2M

ALL other players must pay you $2M each.

💣 Deal Breaker

2 cards • Bank value: $5M

Steal a COMPLETE property set from an opponent (including any house/hotel on it). Wasted if target has no complete set.

🤓 Sly Deal

3 cards • Bank value: $3M

Steal 1 property from opponent's INCOMPLETE set. Cannot take from complete sets.

🔄 Force Deal

4 cards • Bank value: $3M

Swap 1 of your properties for 1 of opponent's (from incomplete sets). No equal value required.

⛔ Just Say No

3 cards • Bank value: $4M

Cancel ANY action against you. Does NOT count as a turn play. Can chain: JSN vs JSN vs JSN.

🏛️ House

3 cards • Bank value: $3M

Add to a COMPLETE property set. Adds $3M to rent. Required before Hotel. Cannot go on Railroad/Utility.

🏘️ Hotel

2 cards • Bank value: $4M

Add to a complete set that ALREADY has a House. Adds $4M to rent (debated: some play $4M replaces $3M house).

✖️2 Double The Rent

2 cards • Bank value: $1M

Must be played WITH a rent card (same turn). Doubles the rent amount. Counts as 1 of your 3 plays. JSN cancels only the double (original rent still owed).

🔄 Turn Flow

1. Draw: Pick up 2 cards from draw pile (5 cards if hand is empty)

2. Play: Up to 3 card plays in any combination:

  • Place money/action cards face-up in your bank
  • Play property cards to your property area
  • Play action cards to the discard pile (triggering their effect)
  • Rearrange your wildcards between sets (free, not a play)

3. Discard: If hand > 7 cards, discard extras to the pile

4. End turn

"A card laid is a card played" — no take-backs. Cards on the table can NEVER return to your hand. You don't have to use all 3 plays.

💰 Payment Rules

Who chooses what to pay with?

The paying player decides. They can use bank money, property, action cards in bank, or houses/hotels. Any combination.

Where does property payment go?

Property paid goes to the opponent's property area (not their bank). Money goes to opponent's bank.

Is change given?

No change. If you overpay, the opponent keeps everything. Strategy: pay with exact amounts when possible.

Can I pay from my hand?

No. Only cards already on the table (bank + property area) can be used for payment.

What if I can't afford it?

Pay everything you can. Opponent gets what you give. You're not eliminated — you just have nothing left on the table.

Action cards used as payment?

If an action card was in your bank (as money), it transfers to opponent's bank as money. They cannot use it as an action.

⛔ Just Say No Rules

Does JSN count as one of your 3 plays?

No. JSN is a free reaction — can be played even on opponent's turn.

Can you JSN a JSN?

Yes. You can chain: Player A plays Deal Breaker → Player B plays JSN → Player A plays JSN (un-does opponent's No) → Player B plays another JSN. All 3 JSN cards could be used in one exchange.

JSN + Double The Rent?

JSN cancels only the Double The Rent. Original rent amount is still owed.

Is the JSN card kept after use?

No. It goes to the discard pile like any other action card.

🏛️ House & Hotel Rules

Order: House MUST be placed before Hotel. Hotel requires existing House on a complete set.

Placement: Only on complete property sets. Cannot go on Railroad or Utility.

Rent bonus: House = +$3M, Hotel = +$4M. (Debated: some play Hotel replaces House for $4M total, others stack for $7M total.)

Stealing: Deal Breaker takes entire set INCLUDING house/hotel. Sly Deal and Force Deal CANNOT take house/hotel from a complete set (but can if it's orphaned on the table).

Orphaned house/hotel: If you pay with property from a set that had a house/hotel, the house/hotel stays on the table until you complete another set.

💣 Deal Breaker Rules

Target: Must target a complete property set. Takes the entire set + any house/hotel on it.

Wasted play: If you play Deal Breaker against someone with NO complete set, the card is discarded and you lose that play. "A card laid is a card played."

Can be blocked: Opponent can play Just Say No to cancel it.

🏠 Property & Wildcard Rules

Can you win with duplicate color sets?

Yes. Any 3 complete sets win, even 3 sets of the same color.

Multi-color (10-color) wildcard rules:

Can be played alone or with other properties. Can be stolen via Sly/Force Deal if NOT in a complete set. Has $0 bank value (can't pay with it). Can only charge rent against it if paired with another property card.

Can wildcards be moved between sets?

Yes, but only during YOUR turn. Two-color wildcards can be flipped. Cards freeze at end of turn.

Overflow properties?

If you have more cards than a set needs (via wildcards), extras start a new set of that color.
